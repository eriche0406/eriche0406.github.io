NFL and NBA Sports Trading Pipeline

Developed a profitable sports trading pipeline that predicts NFL/NBA player performance and betting outcomes. Built automated web scraping to manage large-scale NFL/NBA statistics and designed a custom ensemble model gradient that achieved over 65% accuracy in Over/Under player prop predictions — significantly surpassing the breakeven threshold. Additionally, engineered an optimized betting strategy to manage risk and maintain consistent profitability across varying bankroll sizes.

Currently not open-source. Paper discussing the model's edge and overall pipeline along with historical portfolio results of the sports trading pipeline below.

Technologies Used: Python, Selenium, TensorFlow, MongoDB, Flask, HTML/JS
